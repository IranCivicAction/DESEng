About Us

Detailed Engineering Services Inc provides specialized welding, metallurgical, and industrial equipment engineering support to oil & gas industries, fabricators, manufacturers, inspection organizations, and industrial asset owners. We deliver practical, reliable, and code-compliant engineering solutions tailored to operational, technical, and regulatory requirements.

Our expertise includes pressure welding engineering, in-service welding support, structural welding engineering, CWB certification support, repair welding engineering, fracture mechanisms and failure analysis, crane and elevating device engineering support, Transport Canada highway and portable tank engineering, metallurgy, material selection consulting, and quality and compliance support. Our focus is to help clients improve safety, reliability, integrity, and long-term operational performance.
