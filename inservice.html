In-Service Welding Engineering Support

In-service welding on live pipelines and piping systems is a highly cost-effective solution for maintenance, repair, modification, and hot tapping operations.

However, it is also one of the most technically demanding welding applications in the oil and gas industry, requiring careful engineering assessment, strict safety controls, and code-compliant execution.

Key technical challenges include burn-through risk, hydrogen-induced cracking (HIC), and operational constraints during field execution.

Our services include:

  • Welded sleeve design and support
  • Branch connection engineering
  • Hot tapping procedure development
  • Live pipeline repair engineering
  • Welding procedure qualification (WPQ/WPS support)

We provide engineering support in accordance with CSA Z662, API 1104 Annex B, ASME PCC-2, and other applicable standards.
