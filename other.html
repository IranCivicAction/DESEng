Other Engineering Services

In addition to our specialized welding, structural, pressure equipment, and transportation engineering services, we provide a broad range of technical consulting and compliance support tailored to industrial clients.

Our additional engineering services include:

  • ASME Quality Control Manual Development (CSA B51 compliance)
  • Support with provincial regulators (ABSA, TSASK, ITSM)
  • Industrial failure analysis and root cause investigation
  • Metallurgical assessment and material selection consulting
  • ISO 3834 welding quality system implementation
  • Custom equipment design and fabrication support
  • Engineering review of modifications and special projects

Our focus is to provide practical engineering solutions that improve safety, compliance, reliability, and operational efficiency across industrial systems.

We work closely with manufacturers, fabricators, inspection agencies, and equipment owners to solve complex technical challenges with confidence and precision.
