Crane & Elevating Device Engineering

We provide specialized engineering support, supervision, and certification services for mobile, overhead, and articulating cranes, as well as elevating platforms, lifting devices, and related equipment. Our services support equipment owners, inspection companies, and service providers by providing engineering oversight for annual inspections, structural integrity evaluations, and repair procedure development.

In accordance with applicable industry codes and standards, crane and elevating device inspections must be performed under the supervision of a qualified Professional Engineer. We provide the required engineering review, technical assessment, and certification support to help ensure inspection activities, structural repairs, and maintenance programs are completed safely, efficiently, and in compliance with regulatory requirements.

Our expertise includes engineering support for structural repair procedures, fitness-for-service evaluations, and technical review of inspection findings in accordance with applicable standards such as CSA Z150, CSA Z150.3, CSA Z248, CSA B167, CSA B354.7, CSA B355, and other relevant codes governing lifting and elevating equipment.
