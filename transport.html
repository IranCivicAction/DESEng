Transport Canada Highway & Portable Tank Engineering Support

With the support of our Transport Canada (TC)-registered Design Engineer for Highway and Portable Tanks, we provide specialized engineering support for the design, repair, modification, and regulatory compliance of tank systems.

Our services ensure compliance with CSA B620 and applicable Transport Canada requirements for highway and portable pressure vessels.

We work closely with tank owners, repair facilities, and manufacturers to ensure engineering activities are completed safely, efficiently, and in compliance with regulatory and operational requirements.

Our experience includes support for:

  • TC 406 highway fuel tanks
  • TC 331 / MC 331 ammonia and LPG tanks
  • TC 51 portable nurse tanks
  • TC 60 portable tanks
  • Other regulated highway tank systems

Services include repair procedure development, structural modification support, new tank design assistance, and facility registration support with Transport Canada.

We provide practical engineering solutions focused on safety, compliance, reliability, and long-term service performance.
