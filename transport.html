Transport Canada Highway & Portable Tank Engineering Support

With the support of our Transport Canada (TC)-registered Design Engineer for Highway and Portable Tanks, we provide specialized engineering support for the design, repair, modification, and regulatory compliance of highway and portable tank systems in accordance with CSA B620 and applicable Transport Canada requirements. Our services include repair procedure development, new tank design support, engineering assessment, structural modifications, and facility registration support with Transport Canada. We work closely with tank owners, repair facilities, and manufacturers to ensure engineering activities are completed safely, efficiently, and in compliance with regulatory and operational requirements. Our experience includes developing repair procedures and engineering support for a wide range of tank systems, including TC 406 highway fuel tanks, TC 331/MC 331 highway tanks for ammonia and LPG service, TC 51 portable nurse tanks, TC 60 portable tanks, and other regulated highway and portable tank applications. With extensive experience in pressure-containing and transportation-related equipment, we provide practical engineering solutions that support safe operation, code compliance, repair integrity, and long-term service reliability.
