Pressure Welding Engineering Support

Our Pressure Welding Engineering Support services provide comprehensive technical guidance for the development, qualification, review, and implementation of welding procedures used in pressure-retaining systems and critical fabrication applications.

We support clients in preparing Welding Procedure Specifications (WPS), Procedure Qualification Records (PQR), and Welder Performance Qualifications (WPQ) in accordance with recognized industry codes and standards.

Our services include qualification planning, welding procedure oversight, destructive and non-destructive testing support, and technical coordination with regulatory authorities such as ITSM, ABSA, and TSASK.

With more than 20 years of experience across diverse industrial sectors, we provide practical engineering solutions focused on compliance, efficiency, reliability, and long-term operational value.
